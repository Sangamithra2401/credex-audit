## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. The audit is 100% free, no login required. You see results before we ask for your email. We're a lead-gen tool for Credex — we make money when we help teams with significant savings switch to Credex credits. If your spend is already optimised, we'll say so honestly.

**Q: How accurate is the savings estimate?**
A: The audit uses current public pricing from each vendor's official page (sourced and dated in our codebase). The savings recommendations are rules-based, not AI-generated — a finance person should agree with the math. We err on the side of conservative estimates.

**Q: Do you store my spending data?**
A: The audit inputs (tools, plans, spend) are stored to generate your shareable URL. We don't link this to your identity unless you choose to submit your email. The public share URL strips your email and company name.

**Q: What is Credex?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — for Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise, and others — sourced from companies that overforecast or pivoted. The discount is real. For teams spending more than $500/month on AI tools, a Credex consultation often pays for itself in the first month.

**Q: Why should I trust these recommendations?**
A: Every pricing number in the audit traces to an official vendor URL with a verification date. The logic is public — you can read the audit engine source code on GitHub. If you think a recommendation is wrong, we want to know.
